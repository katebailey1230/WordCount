About WORDCOUNT

This site was created to give you a sense of how many words you're using in a specific line of text. This may be advantageous to you if you are editing a draft of a paper and need to know what words are used too frequently, and other word options to use. This is an open-source project designed using Django 2.02 and Python 3.6. Contributions can, and should, be made to GitHub.
